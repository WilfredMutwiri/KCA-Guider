~ About KCA Location Guider ~

KCA Location Guider is a navigation site tailored for students at KCA university. Its primary purpose is to assist students in finding their way around the school, making it particularly helpful for newcomers and those who are not familiar with the buiding layouts. The site offers step-by-step directions to classrooms, offices and other key locations within the school, ensuring students can navigate confidently and efficiently. With a clean and intuitive interface, this web application simplifies the school experience and helps students reach their destinations without hassle!
